Computer Force FAQ

Here are the lists of FAQ that consumers often asked on Computer Force.

Q1: What is EC2 Auto Scaling?

EC2 Auto Scaling is a fully managed service designed to launch or terminate CF EC2 instances automatically to help ensure you have the correct number of CF EC2 instances available to handle the load for your application. CF EC2 Auto Scaling helps you maintain application availability through fleet management for EC2 instances, which detects and replaces unhealthy instances, and by scaling your CF EC2 capacity up or down automatically according to conditions you define.
